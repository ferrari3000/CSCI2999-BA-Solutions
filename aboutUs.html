Our History

We have had a presence in Columbus, Ohio for 4 decades! M & P Records opened up shop in 1977, and consider ourselves a staple within the Columbus music scene. Our founder, Steve Townshend (no relation), always had an affinity for music and wanted to share it with the world. He cared deeply about the breadth of his music selection, because he believed everyone deserved to experience everything music had to offer.

Keeping Steve's legacy alive today, M&P Records boasts of having one of most diverse record collections around. Sure, we have the classic bands on vinyl that everybody knows- Jimi Hendrix, The Beatles, Pink Floyd, The Who, Rolling Stones, Led Zeppelin...etc, etc. However, we also carry records from almost every mainstream artist that offered one. Bands such as: Beastie Boys, Marvine Gaye, David Bowie, Beck, Daft Punk, Madvillain, Joy Division, Fleet Foxes, and many, many more.

Come see why we're the best in Columbus. Let us live up to our slogan: We know music.
